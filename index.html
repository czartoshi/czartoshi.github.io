About

$MiniCloud uses the power of the cloud combined with IaaS in order to boost its performance when needed. MiniCloud uses a bleeding edge modular strategy system that will be constantly feed by our data scientists to match the needs of the network. $MiniCloud is programmed to recharge Service Fuel while increasing in both liquidity and value.


Minicloud uses the following constraints:

4% tax on transactions
2% goes to holders in order to charge service credit and increase each users profit.
2% is auto-locked to liquidity


Forever Deflationary supply/burn

We will burn more than 45% of the total supply after launch and sent it to a black hole address. As this address also participates in the protocol, it accumulates more tokens, thereby effectively removing them from circulation. There is no limit to the burn, the black hole will keep growing, increasing the scarcity of MINICLOUD. Dev tokens will be burned too. Large investors will have the same chances as individual users on Launch. We will invest 5% of the overall capital in our development. MiniCloud is minable - we will release clients that allow to extend the network as a user. The network will determine the best routes and load balance in a round robin system. If users provide resources the network will provide fuel in return.

Auto-locking liquidity

2% of each transaction is locked to liquidity forever. This contributes to less volatility and a continuously increasing price floor.

100% safe

LP tokens will be burned thereby locking initial liquidity away forever. Fair distribution without whales.
